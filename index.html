F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can any one join Nayak baba Dham trust.?

    Our trust warmly welcomes individuals from all walks of life to be a part of our community. For more information or to join us, please feel free to contact us via email or phone. We look forward to hearing from you and sharing the enriching experiences of our trust.

  • How can I contribute or donate to support the activities of Dham Trust?

    To become a member of our trust, we kindly request a nominal annual fee of ₹365, and you are welcome to contribute any additional amount as per your wish. Your support will help us sustain and further our mission. We appreciate your generosity and commitment to our trust.

  • What services and facilities does Baba Nayak Dham Trust provide to devotees?

    Baba Nayak Dham Trust offers a range of services to devotees, including free accommodation, prasad (blessed food), spiritual counseling, and organized pilgrimages to holy sites. We strive to ensure a comfortable and fulfilling experience for all.

  • Can I make a donation or contribute to the activities of Baba Nayak Dham Trust?

    Yes, Baba Nayak Dham Trust gratefully accepts donations and contributions from individuals who wish to support our noble cause. You can make a contribution in various forms, including monetary donations, volunteering your time and skills, or offering materials required for rituals and events.

  • Are there any eligibility criteria or restrictions to participate in the programs and services of Baba Nayak Dham Trust?

    Baba Nayak Dham Trust is open to all devotees, regardless of age, gender, or background. There are no specific eligibility criteria or restrictions to participate in our programs and services. We embrace diversity and strive to create an inclusive spiritual environment.

  • How can I get in touch with Baba Nayak Dham Trust for further inquiries or to seek guidance?

    You can contact Baba Nayak Dham Trust through various channels, including our dedicated helpline, email, or by visiting our official website. Our dedicated team is available to assist you with any inquiries, guidance, or support you may require.
